# **Specifications: Expert in Sales Process Automation and Competitive Intelligence**

---

## **1\. Project Context and Objectives**

### **1.1 General Context**

Happy House, a player in the tourist accommodation sector, aims to optimize its sales processes and competitive intelligence through automation. The goal is to free up time for sales teams so they can focus on high-value activities: customer relations, negotiation, and closing deals.

### **1.2 Strategic Objectives**

* Reduce time spent on repetitive administrative tasks by **50% to 70%**.  
* Improve sales responsiveness through automated alerts and follow-ups.  
* Optimize competitive intelligence with real-time data collection and analysis.  
* Increase lead conversion rates by **30% to 40%**.  
* Standardize sales processes to guarantee consistent service quality.

---

## **2\. Desired Expert Profile**

### **2.1 Mandatory Technical Skills**

#### **Automation of Competitive Intelligence**

The expert must master the following technologies and methodologies:

* **Web Scraping and Automated Collection**: Ability to set up scrapers to monitor prices, availability, customer reviews, and competitor communications.  
* **Automated Intelligence Tools**: Mastery of tools such as COEXEL, Make Me Win, or equivalent multi-source collection solutions.  
* **AI for Intelligence**: Leveraging AI for semantic analysis, trend detection, and predictive alerts.  
* **Real-time Alert Configuration**: Setting up automatic notification systems for significant competitor movements.  
* **Dashboards and Data Visualization**: Creating dynamic dashboards to visualize intelligence data.

#### **CRM and Sales Process Automation**

The expert must demonstrate expertise in:

* **CRM Platforms**: Mastery of at least two solutions among HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho CRM, or Kommo CRM.  
* **Automated Schedule Management**: Automation of appointment booking, calendar synchronization, and automatic reminders.  
* **Sales Workflows**: Design of automation scenarios for lead qualification, nurturing, and automated follow-ups.  
* **Form and Webinar Management**: Automated processing of registrations, data collection, and post-event enrichment.  
* **Data Integration**: Seamless connection between CRM, marketing tools, ERP, and communication platforms.

#### **No-code and Low-code Automation Tools**

Proven mastery of at least two of the following platforms:

* **Make (formerly Integromat)**: Complex visual workflows, advanced data manipulation.  
* **Zapier**: Rapid automation, large catalog of connectors (8,000+ apps).  
* **n8n**: Open-source solution, self-hosting capability, customizable workflows with scripts.

### **2.2 Methodological Skills**

* **Process Analysis and Audit**: Ability to map existing processes, identify friction points, and find automation opportunities.  
* **Solution Architecture**: Design of custom workflows integrated into the existing tech ecosystem.  
* **ROI Approach**: Ability to quantify expected time and productivity gains.  
* **Project Management**: Steering implementation from A to Z with defined milestones and deliverables.  
* **Training and Support**: Skill transfer to ensure team autonomy.

### **2.3 Personal Qualities**

* Analytical mindset to interpret intelligence data and sales performance.  
* Rigor in data management and automation execution.  
* Teaching skills (pedagogy) to train teams on new tools.  
* Proactivity in identifying new optimization opportunities.  
* Adaptability to rapid technological changes in the sector.

### **2.4 Required Experience**

* Minimum **3 years** of experience in sales process automation.  
* Demonstrable references in automated competitive intelligence.  
* Successful CRM integration experience with full sales cycle automation.  
* Knowledge of the tourism or hospitality sector (major asset).

---

## **3\. Scope of Missions**

### **3.1 Automated Competitive Intelligence**

* **Phase 1: Audit and Scope Definition**: Identify key competitors, define strategic indicators (rates, availability, digital presence), and map relevant data sources.  
* **Phase 2: Technical Implementation**: Configure scraping tools and AI agents to monitor marketing actions; create centralized databases and BI integrations.  
* **Phase 3: Alerts and Reporting**: Set up real-time alerts for triggers (price drops, new offers), dynamic dashboards, and automated weekly/monthly reports.

### **3.2 Sales Support Automation**

* **Schedule Management**: Online booking automation, calendar sync, and automated SMS/email reminders.  
* **CRM Management**: Automated lead creation, data enrichment, scoring workflows, multi-channel prospecting sequences (Email, LinkedIn), and automated quote generation.  
* **Post-Webinar Management**: Automated registration collection, reminder sequences, and lead segmentation based on responses to trigger personalized nurturing.

### **3.3 Integration and Synchronization**

* Seamless connection between intelligence tools, CRM, and webinars.  
* Elimination of double entries via bidirectional synchronization.  
* Scalable architecture for future tool additions.

| Phase | Timeline | Deliverables |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **1\. Diagnosis** | Weeks 1-2 | Audit report, automation opportunity map, detailed technical proposal. |
| **2\. Implementation** | Weeks 3-8 | Configured intelligence and CRM tools, technical documentation. |
| **3\. Training** | Weeks 9-10 | Training sessions, user documentation (guides & FAQ), tutorial videos. |
| **4\. Follow-up** | Months 3-6 | Performance reports, reactive support, and continuous improvement recommendations. |

## **5\. Performance Criteria and KPIs**

### **5.1 Operational Efficiency**

* **Time saved per salesperson**: Minimum 10 hours per week.  
* **Automation rate of repetitive tasks**: Target 80%.  
* **Lead processing time**: 70% reduction.

### **5.2 Sales Performance**

* **Conversion rate increase**: \+30% to 40%.  
* **Qualified appointments**: \+50%.  
* **Sales cycle duration**: \-30%.
